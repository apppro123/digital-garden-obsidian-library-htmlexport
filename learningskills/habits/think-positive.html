Think positive

#disclaimerN regarding 12 rules for life: topic about treating yourself as you would treat somebody you are responsible for

Summary

Thinking positive (especially about yourself) contributes to faster learning and a better health.

Really that bad

Am I really that bad as I always say/think? How many of my bad performances are just talked into me?

LIE

Means "Limited Idea Entertained" and groups all ideas that are limiting you. For example for a long time it was believed that an human cannot run a mile under 4 minutes. But when it was broken once, a lot of people did it.
Discard limiting ideas. And create new true beliefs by finding real evidence. E.g. everytime I did a presentation, was it really bad? Did the audience throw bad vegetables? Or did they applause and somebody said it was really good afterwards?

Stop ANT's

Unfortunately there is not enough ant spray against all these Automatic Negative Thought(s).

Inner critic is a clown

Make your inner critic hilarious. That has two purposes

  • differentiate it from "your own self" ( #remarkN I would say, it is a part of one self, on the other hand we are indeed able to "battle" ourselfes or have different positions. Often times, as life is, it is not one sided and we have hypocritical opinions)
  • easier to surpress

More chances

Positive thoughts opens the mind.

Don't focus on the tiger

When a tiger hunts us, we just focus on escaping. Today there are a lot of these "tigers" and that limits us.

Undone things creates stress

And in these situation, it is even less helpful, if one blames about oneself. Instead work on the solution.

I am

Are very powerful words.
What comes after has a very powerful impact. Because we want to conform to our ideals. It has a great potenzial for motivation.
