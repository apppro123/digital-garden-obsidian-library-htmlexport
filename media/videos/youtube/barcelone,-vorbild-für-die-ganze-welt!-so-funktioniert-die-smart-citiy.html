gather data

A lot of sensors, in bins or in laterns, gather data of the environment: Air quality, noise, count of cars and others. Solar hubs, special laterns, were developed which run with solar energy and have a lot of sensors to tell the city and the responsible people, where action has to be taken, where it is more needed.

  • solar hubs
    • run with solar energy
    • measure a lot of things
  • data is used to evaluate where action is (most) needed
    • like reducing traffic, changing routes

super blocks

Buildings were chosen to build a "super block". There the streets were changed from roads for cars to mainly zones for the residents. Just the redient's cars are allowed to drive with 10km/h. All the other traffic was bypassed around this block. The super block makes the citiy and their citizens healthier through noise and air pollution reduction.

  • cars were changed to pedestrian zones
    • a lot of plants, banks to sit and playground for kids
    • places to meet people and hang out for young and old :)
  • just redisents are allowed to drive with their cars with 10km/h
  • air pollution and noise reduction

expansion of the local public traffic

This change was just possible with the expansion of the local public traffic. Otherwise there would be too much individual traffic in the city for the streets.

  • every citizien which gives up their car, gets 3 years of free local public traffic

solar cars charge houses

Solar cars can be used liked batteries. Because cars stand a lot of the time during the day, then electricity can be used for the houses and its consumption. With the invenvtion of wallbox, an AI/algorithm, electricity can be used where it is needed most. During the day, when the cars stand, it is used in the house. Of course all car shelters have solar panels on the roofs. The algorithm calculates when the car is needed and will charge it/let enough power in it for this duration. At night, when electricity prices are low it will be charded.

  • cars are used as rolling/standing batteries
  • AI calculates where energy is needed most at the moment
  • smart charging
