Summary/First paragraph

Stop using narratives like "mass migration", "good versus bad migrant". They are just feeding right-wing.
And rather improve communication around climate and migration to build safe passages for migration.

Contraproductive migration narrative

  • Speaking about mass migration due to climate change is
    • helping the right-wing agenda to spread fear
    • helping governments make new policies against migration (like surveillance, border control, ...)
    • not helping to combat climate change/mitigation
  • good versus bad migrant
    • good: fleeing from catastrophes
    • bad (less good): economic migrants
  • not helpful to create new ( #remarkN so in generally not divide it? But, with my current knowledge, there are migrants, that are less necessary than others when just resettling because of fulfilling a dream. Not that this is bad, but it's something else than fighting for one's life.) category for ones displaced by climate impacts
    • because it brings up narrative of some have more legitimacy than others to migrate
    • rather integrate it in current global migrations dynamics
  • it's an environmental crisis (only)
    • not an humanitarian crisis
    • "appropriate" response: close borders and population control

Contradictory

Being against migrants is especially contradictory when looking on the high dependence of migrant labour in Europe, US and Australia LabourMigration

Aim

  • improve communication aground climate and migration by
    • building a wider movement understanding
    • dive deeper into the subject area
  • build safe passages for migration with equity, dignity and humanity
    • even though many people don't want to leave their homes, migrations is part of the solution and a form of adaption to climate change
  • climate justice with with mitigation and adaption to impacts of climate change
    • climate movement has to learn working on migration and racial justice issues and support e.g. with sharing resources
    • migration and climate organisation have to work together
