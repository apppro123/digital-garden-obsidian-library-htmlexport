chapter 2: New animals, new plants

#terms Neozoen Neophyten Neobiota Neomyceten

  • often invasive species, which were not intended to be released in a new region but rather hold prison e.g. as a "pet", cause problems
    - Of course not animals or plants are the problem, but only humans which caused the displacement.
    - Problems: new species could extinguish domiciled species
    - especially in poor countries where they have no/less money and know-how to combat Neobiota
    - e.g. in germany 400 of 4000 plants are Neophytes, in urban areas it is even 40-50% of all plants (because these are the centres where introduced species mostly arrive)
    - through climate warming species will spread and also settle in non-human areas
    - Since 1993 there is an agreement for combating Neobiota but just half of the countries are doing something, even though the economic and agricutlurel damages could be really big
    => As long as climate changes (rapidly) flora and fauna will, too and we know little to none about it and its impacts... Especially because all forecasts last to 2050 or maximal to 2100...
